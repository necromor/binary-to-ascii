How to use the converter:

To convert from binary to ascii:
Paste the binary into the left/upper textarea and click the button below. The result of the convertion will be displayed in right/lower textarea.
To convert from ascii to binary:
Paste the ascii into the right/lower textarea and click the button below. The result of the convertion will be displayed in left/upper textarea.
